Introduction

Welcome to the Red Sea Afar Human Rights Organization website. We are pleased to provide you with an introductory overview of the organization. The Red Sea Afar Human Rights Organization is a non-political and non-profit organization. It is a human rights organization concerned with human rights in Eritrea in general and the rights of the Red Sea Afar in particular. Principles and ethics of the organization: The Red Sea Afar Human Rights Organization works with regional and international organizations based on transparency and accountability in all its activities. We address countries and influential people in the country and demand respect for human rights on the basis of international principles and protocols.The Red Sea Afar Human Rights Organization works with complete independence and defends the oppressed and vulnerable in Eritrea and is a loud voice for them in international forumsØŒ this organisation was founded in 2016 in the united kingdom.
